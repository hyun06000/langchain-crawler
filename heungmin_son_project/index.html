Heung-min Son

Heung-min Son is a South Korean professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur and the South Korea national team. Considered one of the best wingers in the world, Son is often considered an icon in his home country and in Asia, and was nominated for the Ballon d'Or in 2019.
